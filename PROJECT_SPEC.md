## Design Requirements
1. Each design is a complete multi-page static site (HTML/CSS/JS)
2. All current content must be included
3. Use the MCS logo and favicon from original-assets/
4. Include Kindermusik logo where Kindermusik is mentioned
5. Responsive (mobile-first)
6. Include a toggle button to switch between original copy and improved copy where improvements are made
7. Include Netlify deployment config (netlify.toml)
8. No build step required — pure static files
9. Each design goes in its own directory: `design-1/`, `design-2/`, `design-3/`

## The Three Designs

### Design 1: "Warm & Playful"
A family-friendly design emphasizing warmth and approachability.
- **Color palette:** Warm yellows, soft oranges, cream/ivory backgrounds, touches of teal
- **Typography:** Rounded, friendly sans-serif (Google Fonts: Nunito or Quicksand for headings, Inter or Open Sans for body)
- **Layout:** Generous whitespace, rounded corners, soft shadows, card-based lesson listings
- **Special elements:** Playful musical note decorations (CSS/SVG), animated instrument icons, a welcoming hero with gradient overlay
- **Vibe:** Like walking into a bright, cheerful music room. Parents feel safe, kids feel excited.
- **Pages:** Home, About, Lessons, Kindermusik, Contact

### Design 2: "Modern & Bold"
Contemporary, high-impact design with strong visual hierarchy.
- **Color palette:** Deep navy (#1a1a2e) or charcoal with vibrant coral (#ff6b6b) or gold (#ffd93d) accents, white text
- **Typography:** Bold geometric sans-serif (Google Fonts: Poppins or Montserrat for headings, Source Sans Pro for body)
- **Layout:** Full-width sections with alternating dark/light, bold section dividers, large hero with parallax-ready background, sticky nav
- **Special elements:** Animated counters (years of experience, students taught), testimonial carousel area, gradient overlays on images
- **Vibe:** Professional music academy that takes education seriously but looks exciting. Think premium.
- **Pages:** Single-page scrolling design with section anchors + separate Kindermusik page

### Design 3: "Elegant & Musical"
Sophisticated design with musical motifs woven into the visual language.
- **Color palette:** Deep royal blue (#1e3a5f), ivory/cream (#faf8f0), gold accents (#c9a94e), soft gray
- **Typography:** Elegant serif + script pairing (Google Fonts: Playfair Display for headings, Great Vibes or Dancing Script for accents, Lato for body)
- **Layout:** Asymmetric layouts, overlapping elements, staff-line decorative borders, musical note SVG patterns as subtle backgrounds
- **Special elements:** Animated treble clef dividers, parallax sheet music background sections, elegant card reveals on scroll
- **Vibe:** A conservatory feel — classical elegance meeting modern web design. Inspires confidence and aspiration.
- **Pages:** Home, About, Programs (combined lessons), Kindermusik, Contact
