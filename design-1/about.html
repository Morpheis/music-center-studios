Our Story

Where Musicians Grow Where Every Musician Finds Their Voice

Music Center Studios offers a unique experience serving the needs of Boise and Meridian area music students of all ages. It is our goal to educate, encourage and inspire functional musicians in an engaging and positive environment. We pride ourselves in teaching to all learning styles through a variety of curriculum activities, hands on theory, improvisation, learning games, technique, ear training and repertoire development. For over a decade, Music Center Studios has been the Treasure Valley's home for musical growth. We create an environment where students of every age feel supported, challenged, and inspired. Our approach blends hands-on theory, improvisation, creative games, and technique building ‚Äî because we know that every student learns differently, and every path to music is valid.

Our teachers are experienced, degreed professionals committed to a positive learning environment with high expectations for success. Our teachers aren't just instructors ‚Äî they're degreed professionals and passionate musicians who set high expectations while keeping the atmosphere warm and encouraging.
