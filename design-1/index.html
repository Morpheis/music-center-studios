Who We Are

Educating, Encouraging & Inspiring Musicians Nurturing Confident, Creative Musicians

Music Center Studios offers a unique experience serving the needs of Boise and Meridian area music students of all ages. It is our goal to educate, encourage and inspire functional musicians in an engaging and positive environment. We pride ourselves in teaching to all learning styles through a variety of curriculum activities, hands on theory, improvisation, learning games, technique, ear training and repertoire development. Curriculum objectives are designed to develop a natural sequence of skills for success, and, most importantly, are fun! At Music Center Studios, we believe music isn't just something you learn â€” it's something you live. Our passionate teachers create joyful, hands-on experiences for students of every age and ability across Boise and Meridian. Through creative games, improvisation, ear training, and technique building, we develop confident, well-rounded musicians. And the best part? Every lesson is designed to be genuinely fun.

For over a decade we've been inspiring students young and old to enjoy the wonderful experience of making music. Our lessons cultivate well rounded musicians that play well with others and have a strong foundation in music reading, steady beat, ear training, and theory. For over a decade, families across the Treasure Valley have trusted us to spark a lifelong love of music. Our students don't just learn to play â€” they learn to listen, collaborate, and express themselves with a strong foundation in music reading, rhythm, ear training, and theory.
