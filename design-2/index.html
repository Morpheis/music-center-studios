About Us

Educate, Encourage & Inspire

It is our goal to educate, encourage and inspire functional musicians in an engaging and positive environment. We pride ourselves in teaching to all learning styles through a variety of curriculum activities, hands on theory, improvisation, learning games, technique, ear training and repertoire development. Curriculum objectives are designed to develop a natural sequence of skills for success, and, most importantly, are fun!

For over a decade we've been inspiring students young and old to enjoy the wonderful experience of making music. Our lessons cultivate well rounded musicians that play well with others and have a strong foundation in music reading, steady beat, ear training, and theory.

Strong Foundations

Sight reading, rhythm, and ear training from the very first lesson.

Positive Peers

Curiosity, creative thinking, and cooperative musicianship.

Inspired by Tech

Interactive, technology-driven music experiences that engage.
