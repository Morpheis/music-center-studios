Our Philosophy

Educate. Encourage. Inspire.

At Music Center Studios, we believe every student deserves a rich, engaging musical education. Our approach weaves together hands-on theory, improvisation, technique, ear training, and repertoire development — carefully sequenced to build natural skills for lasting success. We teach to every learning style, because the best music education is one that meets you where you are.

It is our goal to educate, encourage and inspire functional musicians in an engaging and positive environment. We pride ourselves in teaching to all learning styles through a variety of curriculum activities, hands on theory, improvisation, learning games, technique, ear training and repertoire development. Curriculum objectives are designed to develop a natural sequence of skills for success, and, most importantly, are fun!

Licensed Educators

Where Little Ones Discover the Joy of Music Kindermusik — Having fun is just the beginning!

Our award-winning Kindermusik program nurtures whole-child development during the years most critical to brain growth. Through singing, movement, instruments, and storytelling, children build foundations in social skills, problem-solving, pre-math, and early literacy — all wrapped in the pure joy of music-making.

Having fun is just the beginning! Our award-winning curricula provides an engaging musical learning experience during the years most critical to brain development. For three generations Kindermusik has helped millions of children around the world build a strong foundation for a lifetime of learning.
