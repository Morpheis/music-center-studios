All Ages & Levels

Piano Lessons

Our piano program welcomes students of every age and ability. In groups of up to five, students learn on professional Roland™ and Yamaha™ digital pianos — developing ensemble skills, rhythm, ear training, and sight reading in an environment that's both rigorous and joyful. Whether you're placing your hands on the keys for the first time or refining advanced repertoire, there's a place for you here.

All ages/levels, groups up to 5, Roland™ and Yamaha™ digital pianos, ensemble/rhythm/ear training/sight reading. 1hr, $120/mo, $70/yr materials.

$120 / month  |  1-hour weekly sessions
Ages 4–6

Music for Little Mozarts

Our beloved preschool piano program introduces young musicians (ages 4–6) to the keyboard through the charming world of Mozart Mouse and Beethoven Bear. Using this proven curriculum, students begin reading musical notation within their first semester — building confidence, coordination, and a lifelong love of music through playful, age-appropriate instruction.

Preschool piano (4-6yo), Mozart Mouse & Beethoven Bear curriculum, reading notes in first semester. 1hr, $120/mo, $70/yr materials.

$120 / month  |  1-hour weekly sessions
Beginners to Advanced

Adult Piano

It's never too late to start — or to come back. Our adult piano program serves beginners through advanced players with flexible day and evening class times designed to fit your schedule. Whether you're fulfilling a lifelong dream or picking up where you left off, our supportive group setting makes learning both productive and deeply enjoyable.

Beginners through Advanced, day/evening times. 1hr, $120/mo, $70/yr materials.

$120 / month  |  Day & evening options
Ages 7+

Guitar Lessons

Our guitar program covers it all — technique, chords, jamming, and ear training for students ages 7 and up, including adults. Students build a versatile skill set that lets them play the music they love, whether it's strumming campfire songs or mastering intricate fingerpicking patterns. Our group format adds the fun of playing together.

Ages 7+, technique + chords + jamming + ear training. Adults too. 1hr, $120/mo, $70/yr materials.

$120 / month  |  1-hour weekly sessions

Group Voice Lessons

Our voice program develops confident singers through expert instruction in breathing technique, vocal control, harmony, and stage presence. Students discover the power and beauty of their own voice in a supportive group setting that builds poise and musicianship.

Singing, breathing, harmony, confidence, poise. Group voice lessons building confident performers.
